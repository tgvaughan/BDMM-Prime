Contributing to BDMM-Prime
==========================

The BDMM-Prime project maintainers gladly accept help with the
improvement of the software.  However be aware that we cannot accept
all suggestions for changes, even if you've already implemented these
changes and are happy with them yourself. In particular, the
maintainer reserves the right to reject changes that do not align with
their vision for the project.

To avoid dissapointment, please follow these guidelines:

1. Raise an issue indicating the problem or feature you
would like to address and wait for feedback from the
maintainer.
2. Once the maintainer has agreed that they would be
happy to accept a fix or a feature implementation, 
implement your proposed chnages.
3. Once complete, commit your changes to a new branch
in your local repository, then open a pull request.
4. The maintainer will review your changes (please be
patient) and either request/make modifications or
merge into the repository.
